Deparures Nov







Day 1

Arrive Delhi

Arrive Delhi station / airport and get transfer to hotel by your own. Today the day is free for shopping / sightseeing (at your own). Night halt at Delhi.

Day 2

Delhi - Mussorie

After breakfast proceed to Mussoorie (8-10 hrs. journey). Lunch will be served en route. Arrive Mussoorie & check in at hotel. Evening free for rest. Night halt at Mussoorie.

Day 3

Mussorie

After breakfast visit the famous Kempty Water Falls by Cable Car (at our cost). After lunch visit Mussoorie Lake. Night halt at Mussoorie.

Day 4

Mussorie – Haridwar

After breakfast proceed to Haridwar 2 hrs journey. On arrival visit the Famous Har ki Paudi, on Ganga Ghat to take part in the Ganga Aarti. Night halt at Haridwar.

Day 5

Haridwar – Nainital (Bhimtal)

After breakfast proceed to Nainital.(235 kms /8 hrs journey) On arrival day will be free at leisure. Night halt at Nainital (Bhimtal).

Day 6

Nainital (Bhimtal)

After lunch full day sightseeing tour of Ranikhet to enjoy the scenic beauty of the Golf Ground (5200 feet). Ranikhet is headquarter of kumaon Army Regiment, thereafter visit Choubatiya Garden & return back to the hotel. Night stay at Nainital. (Bhimtal).

Day 7

Nainital (Bhimtal)

After breakfast excursion of Nainital Lake Tour which includes Naini Lake, Sattal, Navkuchiya Tal, Bhimtal etc (Boating at your own cost), cable car (at your own cost). In the evening free time for photography / shopping. Night halt at Nainital.

Day 8

Nainital (Bhimtal) - Corbett

After breakfast proceed to Corbett Park (2 hrs. journey). In the evening enjoy the natural scenic beauty of this exotic place. Night halt at Corbett.

Day 9

Corbett

After breakfast proceed to enjoy Jungle Safari from Seetavani / Jhirna Gates (at our cost). Jim Corbett National Park is rich in flora and fauna, animals, including tigers, leopards and wild elephants on the banks of Ramganga Reservoir, the Sonanadi zone is home to elephants and leopards, along with hundreds of species of bords. Night halt at Corbett.

Day 10

Corbett – Delhi Drop

Early morning proceed to Delhi (8 hrs.journey) passengers will be dropped at Delhi station by 5.00 p.m. with memorable experience of the tour conducted by Heena Tours & Travels.


This tour starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 10.

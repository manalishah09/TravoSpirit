Day 1

Arrive Hyderabad

Arrive Hyderabad station / airport and reach our hotel directly by own. On arrival check in at hotel & freshen up. Day will be free at leisure to relax. Night halt at Hyderabad.

Day 2

Hyderabad - Ramoji Film City – Hyderabad

After breakfast proceed to explore Ramoji Film City (at our cost) with 1633 acres, it is the largest integrated film city in the world, it holds the Guinness World Records for being the largest film studio complex in the world and measures 2000 acres. It is also a popular tourism and recreation centre, containing natural and artificial attractions including an amusement park. The Outer Ring Road Phase 2 has acquired land at the entrance and is set to intersect the city. Lunch will be at Ramoji. Evening is free for shopping, return back to hotel. Night halt at Hyderabad.

Day 3

Hyderabad

After breakfast local sightseeing of Hyderabad which includes Charminar (From Bus), Salar Jung Museum, Birla Temple. After lunch visit to Hussain Sagar Lake, NTR Gardens, which is one of the best garden in Hyderabad City. Evening enjoy the Laser Show (at our cost) which shows culture of Hyderabad. Overnight stay at hotel

Day 4

Hyderabad

After breakfast Transfer to Hyderabad Station / airport (on your own) with memorable experience of Heena Tours & Travels.


This Tour starts with Lunch on Day 1 & ends with breakfast on Day 4.

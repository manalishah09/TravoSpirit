Departures Sep, Oct, Nov, Dec, Jan, Feb, Mar







Itenary

Day 1

Arrive Port Blair

On arrival at Port Blair airport, you will be met and transferred to your hotel, Check in at our hotel & make yourself comfortable. After lunch. In the Evening visit Cellular Jail followed by Son et Lumeiere (Light & Sound) show at Cellular Jail. Return back to your hotel. Dinner and night halt at Port Blair. Note : Cellular Jail is closed on Monday, and National Holidays. Entry to Jail closes by 16:00 hrs. And Light and Sound show is at 18:00 hrs. Tickets available on first come first serve basis.

Day 2

Port Blair To Havelock Island (54 Kms)

In the morning you will be escorted to Port Blair Harbor to board Luxury cruise (1 ½ hrs) or Govt. Ferry (2 ½ hrs) which will take you to HAVELOCK ISLAND. Havelock Island: The Island is named after a British Army Officer. The island offers state-of-the-art facilities for snorkeling and scuba diving. Havelock is famous for its white sand and crystal clear and transparent waters. At the Harbour our representative will receive you and will help you to check in at your pre booked hotel / resort @ Havelock, and then he will escort you to Radhanagar Beach which is Asia’s 7th most beautiful beach. At Radhanagar Beach you can go for swimming or just get your feet wet and admire the serene beauty of the beach, Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 3

In Havelock Island

After Breakfast full day excursion to Elephant Island This Island is located at a distance of around 10kms from the Havelock or 30 mints boat ride from the jetty. It is known for its beautiful corals and exotic life. Here one can enjoy the Water sports, snorkeling etc, but the expense will be borne by the tourists. Evening back to Resort / hotel over night at Havelock.

Day 4

Havelock Island To Port Blair

After Breakfast we take a Ferry to Port Blair, on arrival start for a tour of places, in and around the City, Anthropological Museum, Fisheries Museum, Naval Marine Museum, overnight at Port Blair.

Day 5

Coral Island and Ross Island – Port Blair

After breakfast start off for a full day excursion to Coral Island, (North Bay), to see Coral Reefs, exotic varieties of beautifull Ornamental Fishes, and other Marine Life. This is an ideal place, for snorkeling. Then visit Ross Island also known as Paris of the East. Return to Port Blair and night halt.

Day 6

Departure from Port Blair

Check out the hotel and proceed to Airport. And end of memorable Tour Conducted by Travospirit Tours & Travels.

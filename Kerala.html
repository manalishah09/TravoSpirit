BEST OF KERALA

10 Nights in Hotel

  • Cochin
  • Munnar
  • Thekkady
  • Kumarakom
  • Kanyakumari
  • Poovar
INR 90500.00 (Per Couple)

Deparures Oct,Nov,Dec,Jan,Feb,March







Day 1

Arrive Cochin

Arrive Cochin Station / Airport and reach our hotel directly (Hotel Check/in:- 12:00 noon)& rest the of the day is free for rest / shopping. Night halt at Cochin.

Day 2

Cochin - Munnar

After breakfast visit Dutch Palace, St. Francis Church and Chinese Fishing Nets. After lunch proceed to Munnar (5-6 hrs. journeys), en route visit the famous waterfalls and continue towards Munnar. Night halt at Munnar.

Day 3

Munnar

After breakfast visit Metupatty Dam for Speed boating& Coconut Water (at our cost) and Rose Garden. After lunch visit Hydel Park. Night halt at Munnar.

Day 4

Munnar

After breakfast visit Rajmala view point the highest point of Kerala. After lunch the day is free to explore the natural beauty of Munnar. Night halt at Munnar. Note: Munnar - Eravikulam National Park Closed for 2 Months in a year due to the calving season, in this period we Visit to Tea Museum.

Day 5

Munnar - Thekkady

After breakfast proceed to Thekkady (5 hrs. journey), en route visit the Tea Gardens of Munnar. Arrive Thekkady and check-in at hotel. In the evening take a guided Spice Plantation tourand Kerala Traditional Kathakali show (at our Cost).Night halt at Thekkady.

Day 6

Thekkady - Kumarakom

After breakfast free time for boating at Periyar Lake OR Jeep Safari (at your own cost) / shopping etc. After lunch proceed to Kumarakom (6 hrs. journey). Night halt at Kumarakom.

Day 7

Kumarakom

Morning free to enjoy resort facilities. After lunch enjoy Kerala famous back waters ride for 3 hrs (at our Cost).. Night halt at Kumarakom.

Day 8

Kumarakom - Kanyakumari

After breakfast proceed to Kanyakumari (10 hrs. journey), evening visit to Gandhi Mandap, Triveni Sangam Ghat and Sunset Point. Night halt at Kanyakumari.

Day 9

Kanyakumari - Poovar Island

Morning view the Sunrise from the hotel Terrace, after breakfast visit to Vivekananda Rock Memorial(Special Pass by Heena).After lunch proceed to Poovar (3 hrs. journey). Evening is free at leisure. Night halt at Poovar Island.

Day 10

Poovar Island - Trivandrum - Poovar Island

After breakfast visit to PadmanabhSwamy Temple & Kovlam famous Beach, after lunch enjoy at beach. Night halt at Poovar Island.

Day 11

Train Journey

After breakfast transfer to Trivandrum railway station / Airport by Heena to board your train/Flight for Mumbai with memorable experience of the tour conducted by Heena Tours & Travels. Rest Passengers departing by Air/Rail has to transfer to at their own expense.


This tour starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 11.

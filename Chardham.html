Itenary

Day 1

Delhi-Haridwar

Arrive New Delhi by 12:00 and reach on your own at our pickup point. After lunch proceed for Haridwar (200 kms, approx 7/8 hrs). Night halt at Haridwar.

Day 2

Haridwar-Rishikesh-Haridwar

After breakfast, full day sightseeing of Rishikesh which includes Laxmanjhula, Ramjhulla. Parmarthdham, Swargasram Geeta Bhavan and cross the ganges by boat. After lunch visit to the Famous Har ki Paudi, on Ganga Ghat, to take part in the Ganga Aarti. Night halt at Haridwar.

Day 3

Haridwar-Barkot (Yamunotri)

After breakfast proceed for Barkot. (196 Kms, approx 8 / 10 hrs) enroute visit the famous Kempty waterfalls at Mussoorie (from Bus) & Night halt at Kharadi (Barkot) (Yamunotri).

Day 4

Barkot – Yamunotri – Barkot

Early morning 04:00 A.M. leave for Jhankichatti (52kms), from here 6km. trekking for Yamunotri. Passengers can opt for Doli / Horse (at your own cost) or by walk. (6500ft.) Night halt at Kharadi (Barkot) (Yamunotri).

Day 5

Barkot – Uttarkashi

After Breakfast leave for Uttarkashi (100 Kms, approx 4-5 hrs.).After lunch visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple. Night halt at Uttarkashi.

Day 6

Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi (200 Kms)

Early Morning 04:00 A.M. proceed to Gangotri. After breakfast visit to Gangotri Temple. An return to Uttarkashi, enroute we stop for a Holy Dip at Gangnani Hot Water Springs. Night halt at Uttarkashi.

Day 7

Uttarkashi - Sitapur(Kedar)

After breakfast proceed to Kedarnath (270 Kms, approx 10-11 hrs.) enroute visit union of Alaknanda and Mandakini rivers.(From the Bus). Night halt at Sitapur.

Day 8

Sitapur – Kedarnath

After breakfast leave for Gaurikund by Local Share a Taxi. Reach Gaurikund & start trek or Horse Riding or Doli (at your own cost) one way for Kedarnath 16 kms. (11750 ft.) Perform Pooja & Darshan at Kedarnathji Temple one of the 12 Jyotirlings. Night halt at Kedarnath. ( In case party Books Helicopter, taxi to Helipad is own Cost and if the Helicopter does not fly due to weather Conditions, Accomodation and Food cost for stay in Sitapur, will have to be paid by Party. (In Kedarnath Food will be simple menu) Only 1 Bucket Hot water will be provided here for Bath.

Day 9

Kedarnath – Sitapur

After Breakfast proceed to Sitapur. After lunch visit holy place where Trijugi Narayani and God Shankar - Parvati got married.(Subject to Road Condition and Traffic) Night halt at Sitapur.

Day 10

Sitapur - Badrinath

Early morning at 4.00am. leave by bus for Badrinath. (253 Kms, approx 13-14hrs.) (10,350ft.) Night halt at Badrinath.

Day 11

Badrinath

Morning take a holy dip at Badrinath hot water springs and darshan. Evening free for local sightseeing of Mana Village near China border. Night halt at Badrinath.

Day 12

Badrinath- Garhwal Srinagar

After fast leave for Garhwal Srinagar (220 Kms, approx 10-12 hrs.) en route enjoy the cable ride at Auli (at your own cost). Night halt at Garhwal Srinagar.

Day 13

Garhwal Srinagar – Haridwar

After breakfast proceed for Haridwar (140 Kms, approx 6-7 hrs.) En route enjoy the adventurous white water rafting (at your own cost). If anyone wish to visit Mata Chandi devi & Mansha Devi temple you can go at your own cost. Night halt at Haridwar.

Day 14

Haridwar-Delhi

After breakfast proceed for Delhi (200 Kms, approx 7/8 hrs) & Tour ends with memorable experience with Heena Tours & Travels.

This Tour starts with Lunch on Day 01 & ends with Lunch Day 14.

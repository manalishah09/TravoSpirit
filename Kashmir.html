Deparures Oct,Nov,Dec,Mar,Apr







Itenary

Day 1

Jammu

Arrive Jammu station / airport and reach to our hotel at your own. Night halt at Jammu. (Hotel check - in time at 12.00 noon). Passenger who are coming by Flight can join the tour from Srinagar from Day 02 directly.

Day 2

Jammu - Pahalgam

After breakfast proceed to Pahalgam (approx 8-10 hrs. journey). Pahalgam is situated at 7,200 feet above sea level on the banks of Lidder River, which is also known as the most scenic hill station of India. Arrive and transfer to Hotel. Evening enjoy the natural beauty. Night halt at Pahalgam.

Day 3

Pahalgam

After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi(at our cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley and Betaab Valley. Enjoy horse riding at Baisaran Valley the beautiful place (at your own cost). Evening is free for shopping / rest. Night halt at Pahalgam.

Day 4

Pahalgam - Gulmarg

After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg (6hrs. journey). It is situated at 8825 feet above Sea level.. Evening free for shopping / rest. Night halt at Gulmarg.

Day 5

Gulmarg - Srinagar

After breakfast take a walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station to enjoy Gandola Cable Car Ride (at our cost upto 1st phase) Enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. After lunch proceed to Srinagar (3 hrs journey). Night halt at Srinagar.

Day 6

Srinagar – Sonmarg - Srinagar

After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg (If road is open / Subject to Military Permission), which is located at 81 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Sonmarg means “meadow of gold” Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point (at your own cost).Evening return back to Srinagar. Night halt at Srinagar.

Day 7

Srinagar

After breakfast check-out from the Hotel enjoy Shikara Ride for 2 hrs. (at our cost), check-in to Houseboat, after lunch sightseeing tour of Srinagar which includes, Shalimar Baug & Nishat Baug. Night halt at Srinagar.

Day 8

Srinagar – Katra (Vaishnodevi)

After breakfast proceed to Katra (10-12 hrs. journey). Night halt at Katra.

Day 9

Katra (Vaishnodevi)

Full day free for Holy Darshan of Mata Vaishnodevi by Horse/Dolly/Helicopter (at your own cost). Night halt at Katra.

Day 10

Katra – Jammu Station Drop

After breakfast we will proceed to Jammu station at 10:00 am& Jammu Airport at 11:00 am (2 hrs. journey) Passengers having early or late flight/train can make their own travel arrangements. Tour ends with Memorable tour conducted by Heena Tours and Travels.

This tour starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 10.
